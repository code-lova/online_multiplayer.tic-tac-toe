Simple Interface and Gameplay!

Tic-tac-toe game usually easy to access from any device and pretty to understand and play. Its a three-by-three grid by two players, who alternately place the marks X and O in one of the nine spaces in the grid. There is no universally agreed rule as to who plays first, but in this article the convention that X plays first is used. Players soon discover that the best play from both parties leads to a draw.
